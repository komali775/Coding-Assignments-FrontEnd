Paragraph on nature

Nature is the source of our living, providing us with food, clothing, and shelter.

Nature is a protective layer that guards us against all kinds of damages and harms.

Nature is a natural cycle that ensures the smooth functioning of every living being.

Nature is beautiful and diverse, and people have always appreciated and valued it.

Nature is under threat from deforestation, pollution, and over-exploitation of natural resources.

It is essential to reduce biological damage, reuse things, and recycle existing materials to create new ones.

We should avoid over-dependence on fossil fuels like coal, petroleum products, and firewood.

We should start using non-conventional sources of energy like the sun, biogas, and wind.

We should check deforestation and take up the planting of trees at a massive rate.
